Fitts Law Study: Dominant vs Non-Dominant Hand

Input, Interaction, & Accessability Assignment 2

Authors: Allison Rodenbaugh, Allison MacMillan, Marissa Tracy, Story Kiser

Summary:

For this Fitts Law Study, we tested the difference in using a dominant hand versus a non-dominant hand on a simple trackpad-movement task. We tested three different right handed users' ability to move a mouse using the track pad and click different objects of varying widths and amplitudes. Each test subject did 9 sessions of 15 clicks per hand. The users first did the task with their dominant hand (right) and then with their non-dominan hand (left).
